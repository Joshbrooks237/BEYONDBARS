## Common Issues & Solutions

### "It doesn't understand me"
- **Speak slower and clearer**
- **Reduce background noise**
- **Check your microphone settings**
- **Try shorter sentences**

### "It's in the wrong language"
- **Make sure you're on the right page** (English vs Spanish)
- **Check browser language settings**
- **Click the language toggle**

### "The button doesn't work"
- **Check microphone permissions** - Browser needs access
- **Try a different browser** - Use Chrome or Edge
- **Refresh the page**

### "Words are missing"
- **Speak louder** - Mic might not hear you
- **Check microphone** - Is it working?
- **Try again** - Click stop and restart
