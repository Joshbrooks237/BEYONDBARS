### 1. AI-Powered Letter Scanner ü§ñ
**The game changer** - helps families write compliant messages

- **Real-time scanning** using OpenAI GPT-4
- **Compliance scoring** (0-100)
- **Smart flagging** of problematic content
  - High severity: Gang activity, escape plans, threats
  - Medium severity: Drug references, weapons
  - Low severity: Contact info, minor issues
- **Alternative phrasing suggestions**
- **Compassionate guidance** - helps you say what you mean safely
- **Quick scan** as you type (warns before full scan)

**Why it matters**: Other services just reject your message. We help you fix it BEFORE sending.

### 2. Commissary Wallet üí∞
**Multiple payment options** - use what you already have

- **Cash App** ‚≠ê (2.5% fee) - Most popular

- **Credit/Debit Cards** (2.9% fee) - Visa, MC, Amex, Discover
- **Bank Transfer/ACH** (0.8% fee) - Lowest fees, takes 2-3 days

**Our fees vs Competition:**

**Example**: Send $100
- JPay: $107 total
- Prison Love (Cash App): $102.50 total
- **You save $4.50 per transaction!**
